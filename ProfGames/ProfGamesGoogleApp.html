ProfGames

Live stream production tools by Profware, Inc.

ProfGames is a desktop production engine for live streamers. It connects a creator's own stream to their OBS scenes so that moments of viewer support become moments on screen.

What it does

We watch for Super Chats, and then use those Super Chats to drive OBS movements, animations, and scene changes — like Streamer.bot, but in a new way. When a viewer sends a Super Chat, the engine can swap a background, play an animation, or trigger an on-screen event in the creator's broadcast, in real time.

How it's used

Each streamer runs ProfGames on their own machine and signs in with their own account. The engine only acts on the creator's own live broadcasts, and everything it produces is rendered locally into that creator's OBS scenes.
